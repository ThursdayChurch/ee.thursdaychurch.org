Connections

The Connection Team at JourneyChurch is one of the best places for you to help make a big place feel small. While serving on the Connection Team, you'll get to meet a variety of people while trying to make people feel comfortable and meeting the needs of our guests each and every week. The main heart and vision of the Connection Team is to do anything and everything to remove barriers so people can be tuned in to what the Lord has for them.
