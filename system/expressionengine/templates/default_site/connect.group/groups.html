Find A Group

Life is not meant to be lived alone. In fact, God hates loneliness. We like to say that real community happens in circles, not in rows. You were created to experience life with other people, and that’s why at JourneyChurch.tv, we encourage you to get into a small group.

Browse Groups
I Need Help

A life group is a group of three or more people who gather each week in a home, workplace, or at the church. In a group you’ll hang out, study the Word, and pray together.

Groups come in all shapes and sizes — married couples, singles, men, women, kids, no kids, workplace, under 20, over 40 — the list goes on. No matter where you are in life, there’s a group for you.

Ready to dive in? Find a group that fits you or start one of your own. Need help? Email lindsey.truelove@journeychurch.tv.

Connect Your Group

Life groups have one, simple purpose: to bring people together. We believe God created us to live in relationship with others and only then can we live the full life He intends for us. Sharing life through community is part of our design, but meaningful relationships aren’t always easy to find. That’s why life groups exist—to make these life-changing relationships relevant and accessible to you.
