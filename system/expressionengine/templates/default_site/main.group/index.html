Who are we?

JourneyChurch is a house where God is honored, where prayers are answered, hearts are mended and lives are changed.

{!--We are a house committed to transforming our cities... one life at a time. A house that uses creativity to point people to the Creator. A house that believes the God of generations past is the answer for the next generation.

The people of this house gather to build healthy relationships and have a blast together. And the highest calling of our house is to worship the God of the universe. The King of kings. The Lord of lords. We are a house. We are JourneyChurch.--}
