Stories

They Change Us

It is a wonderful thing to remember God’s handiwork in our lives, to trace His fingerprints across the details of our faith.


As we move through each season in life we strive to live each moment, each day. Those days define and mold us. They become our milestones for reminding us to keep on. They are the stories of our lives; the testimonies of Christ’s work in finishing our faith.


Stories can encourage those who hear them; they discover they’re not alone. The foundation of their faith is strengthened, and they are emboldened to deepen that faith.


As a church we want to give you the opportunity to encourage others through your story
